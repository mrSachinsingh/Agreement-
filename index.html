Love Agreement

This Agreement is made on July 15, 2024, by and between:

Sachin Pratap Singh

Subhashree Pradhan

Purpose of the Agreement

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a mutual understanding and commitment regarding the expectations and boundaries in our relationship to ensure trust, fidelity, and transparency.

1. Fidelity and Commitment

- Both partners commit to being faithful to each other and not engaging in romantic or sexual relationships with any other individuals.

- If either partner is found to be cheating or in love with another person, this will be considered a breach of this Agreement.

- If either partner is found cheating, both agree not to see each other anymore after the breach is discovered.

2. Online Activity

- Both partners agree to be transparent about their online activities and not to engage in conversations or maintain accounts for the purpose of interacting with other potential romantic interests.

- If either partner is found to be consistently online but busy with accounts or interactions with other individuals, this will be considered a breach of this Agreement.

3. Transparency and Communication

- Both partners agree to maintain open and honest communication with each other about their feelings, concerns, and activities.

- Any suspicion or evidence of the above-mentioned behaviors should be discussed openly to avoid misunderstandings.

4. Separation Clause

- If any of the above terms are breached at any point in time, whether after 10 years or 20 years, both partners agree to separate and live their own lives independently.

- The decision to separate will be made mutually and amicably, ensuring respect and dignity for both parties.

Duration of the Agreement

This Agreement is effective from the date of signing and will remain in effect until either partner breaches the terms, leading to separation as mentioned above.
